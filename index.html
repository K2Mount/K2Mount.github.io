杨翥成 YANG, Zhucheng

Dr. YANG, Zhucheng received his B.S. degree in Chemistry from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (2020). He obtained his Ph.D. from the National University of Singapore (2025) under the supervision of Prof. Jianping Xie, where he is currently continuing as a Research Fellow. His research has been published in Advanced Materials, ACS Nano, CCS Chemistry, Chemical Science, and other journals. His current interests focus on smart synthesis and ligand programming for functional nanomaterials, with an emphasis on understanding atomic-level synthetic chemistry and structure–property relationships.
